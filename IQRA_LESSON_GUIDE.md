# Iqra Lesson Expansion Guide

This guide documents the expanded Iqra lessons for early readers (ages 3–9).
The additions focus on clear letter recognition in isolation, connected forms,
and gradual blending toward fluent reading readiness.

## Lesson Progression Overview

1. **Iqra 1**: Basic Arabic letter recognition (isolated letters)
2. **Iqra 2**: Vowel marks (harakat) with letters
3. **Iqra 3**: Word formation with harakat
4. **Iqra 4**: Three-letter word reading
5. **Iqra 5**: Letter forms by position (beginning, middle, end)
6. **Iqra 6**: Blending practice and short words

## New Lesson Focus Areas

### Iqra 5: Letter Forms by Position
- Standalone vs connected letter forms are shown side-by-side.
- Learners see how letters change at the beginning, middle, and end of words.
- Example cards highlight common letters used in early Quranic reading.

### Iqra 6: Blending and Short Words
- Step-by-step blending prompts guide children to combine sounds.
- Two-letter and three-letter blending patterns reinforce phonetic flow.
- Short, age-appropriate words support early reading accuracy.

## Accessibility & Clarity

- Each lesson uses clear section labels in Arabic and English.
- Standalone and connected forms are color differentiated.
- Large, legible Arabic typography supports young learners.

## Deployment Notes

- Existing Iqra lessons (1–4) remain intact and backward compatible.
- New lessons are added as separate HTML pages and linked from the home page.
